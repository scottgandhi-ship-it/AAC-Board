## Implementation Priority Tiers

### P0 -- Implement Immediately (Life Safety)
1. Emergency phrases: I fell, I'm choking, I'm having chest pain, I can't move, I'm bleeding, I need the nurse
2. Pain quality words: sharp, dull, burning, throbbing + left side/right side
3. Pain trend phrases: it's getting worse, it just started, the medicine isn't helping
4. Toileting urgency: I need to go now, I had an accident, I need to be changed
5. Swallowing safety: I'm choking, food is stuck, it won't go down, I'm coughing when I eat
6. Medication safety: I already took it, I didn't take it yet, it makes me feel sick
7. Fall prevention: I fell, I almost fell, I'm unsteady, lock the wheels
8. Autonomy defense: I said no, stop touching me, ask me first

### P1 -- Implement Before Launch (Daily Independence)
1. Bathing/hygiene supplies: shower, shampoo, lotion, deodorant, dentures
